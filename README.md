# social_media_trends_analysis
An analysis of how Tiktok has quickly become the news outlet of choice among young Americans, a trend applicable mostly to Generation Z.
Users from Generation Z are fifteen percent more likely to use tiktok for news related purposes than users 65 and up. This large gap is necessary to analyze, and using social media user demographics compared with the percentage of users who get their news from social media sites, it reveals this trend. 
This data is sourced from Pew Research who published their findings in an article highlighting varying ages of use in social media useage. This data was collected through a survey conducted August 18-25th,2025.
https://www.pewresearch.org/journalism/fact-sheet/social-media-and-news-fact-sheet/
My methodology was to utilize functions to create various charts to visualize said data and make effective comparisons. 
This study only asked respondents who already used each platform and they only represent english speakers. This data does not reveal media literacy, trust levels, or consumption patterns.
